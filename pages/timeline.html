Sputnik 1

4 October 1957

was the first artificial Earth satellite. It was launched into an elliptical low Earth orbit by the Soviet Union on 4 October 1957 as part of the Soviet space program.

Vostok 1

12 April 1961

Vostok 1 was the first spaceflight of the Vostok programme and the first human orbital spaceflight in history.

Mariner 2

27 August 1962

Mariner 2 was the first robotic space probe to report successfully from a planetary encounter.

Apollo 8

21 December 1968

Apollo 8 was the first crewed spacecraft to leave Earth's gravitational sphere of influence, and the first human spaceflight to reach the Moon.

Apollo 11

20 July 1969

Apollo 11 marked the first time that humans landed on the Moon.

Mars 3

28 May 1971

Mars 3 lander became the first spacecraft to attain a soft landing on Mars

Mars Pathfinder

28 May 1971

Mars Pathfinder was the first rover to operate outside the Earth-Moon system.

Cassini-Huygens

15 October 1997

Cassini was the fourth space probe to visit Saturn and the first to enter its orbit, where it stayed from 2004 to 2017.

Perseverance rover

30 July 2020

Perseverance is a car-sized Mars rover designed to explore the Jezero crater on Mars
